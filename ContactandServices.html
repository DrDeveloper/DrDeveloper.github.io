Services & Prices

Quick Scrape 50$ per square foot -
With our trusty scrapers we will search high and low for whatever stickiness ails you and scrape away until it is no longer a problem. This is mostly an aesthetic cleaning as there may be a bit of tar-like residue left behind. This should clear up, but if not our agents can offer recommendations for follow up cleaning.
Deep Clean 75$ per square foot -
We not only perform a thorough scraping, but we also spritz and spray the area with our patented cleaners, ensuring nothing is left behind. Comes with fresh scents like: New Home, Lemon Rain, and Lavender
Full Service 100$ per square foot -
There's no stopping us. We will perform a steam cleaning after. Very important if the gum has been there for a while and left any level of mold or has hardened the surface its stuck to as this steam cleaning will not only pick up any residue but restore any carpet pliability and softness.
